About DurraClad

At DURRACLAD, we specialize in delivering superior safety uniforms designed for the most demanding industries across the GCC. Born from a commitment to safety, quality, and innovation, Duraclad combines cutting-edge fabric technology with functional design to provide high-performance workwear that keeps your team protected and comfortable on the job.

With a deep understanding of regional industry needs, we aim to set new standards in safety apparel through meticulous craftsmanship, customization options, and timely service. From oil fields to factory floors, Duraclad is your trusted partner in protection.
